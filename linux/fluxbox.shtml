Fluxbox styles

Fluxbox is a minimalistic window manager for X, the standard windowing system on most UNIX operating systems. I haven't used it since high school, but it's a nice piece of software. Right before I stopped using it — the summer before I left for college — I created a series of semi-popular themes (or styles, as the Fluxbox people call them). They all come with the wallpapers after which they were named, with permission from the original author to distribute freely.
